How to Play?

Sketchy Fighters is a 2 player fighting game. Each fighter uses the controls below. They both have an attack button that deals damage and they can track their remaining life with the health bar above them. Once it hits zero, they lose. Last one standing is declared VICTORIOUS!


Player Red Controls


W = JUMP

A = LEFT

D = RIGHT

G = ATTACK

Player Blue Controls


Up Arrow = JUMP

Left Arrow = LEFT

Right Arrow = RIGHT

/ = ATTACK
